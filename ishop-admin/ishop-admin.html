Impact
✳ Unifying fragmented tools into one platform reduced daily tool-switching overhead by 75% — proving that operational efficiency gains come from structure, not just features.
✳ Surfacing data through trends and alerts, rather than raw numbers, gave the ops team faster visibility into revenue and stock — contributing to +18.6% revenue growth in the first month.
✳ Redesigning the restock workflow from 7 steps to 3 showed that the biggest UX wins often come from removing steps, not adding them.
